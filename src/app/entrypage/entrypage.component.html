Our mission is to empower you, to help take your community to greater heights by making it easier to find people with hidden talent grow in their careers, and to help easily communicate your opinion to decision makers.







For a candidate, you can vote on their performance on 15+ issues (Guns, Abortion, Immigration, Inflation, Crime, COVID, Climate, Veterans, Farmers, Seniors, etc.) and on 20+ US Senate or US House committees (Banking, Health, Agriculture, etc.) that they are a member of.

You can also choose which committees you would like candidates to serve in the 2023 Congress session.

Discuss issues, find, and implement solutions as a group with new found friends, on SoWhat Vote, from across the country.

Become a mentor or mentee in your professional or educational field to help your community members grow in their career.







We were inspired by the statewide amendment proposals that are on an election ballot. Based on all the information the person has to choose a Yes or No. We have replicated this simplicity of decision making at SoWhat Vote! with a Yea or a Nay.
